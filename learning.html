A Complete Video Guide for Beginners and Beyond.

A comprehensive guide tailored specifically to beginners in the field of video production. This guide offers a step-by-step approach, providing beginners with the fundamental knowledge and skills necessary to create high-quality videos. With clear instructions and practical tips, it aims to demystify the video production process and empower individuals who are new to this art form. Whether you have no prior experience or limited knowledge, this guide will serve as your go-to resource for mastering the essentials of video creation.

Start Your Videography Journey Now.

Ep 1: From Still to Motion? Get familiar with necessary display information in Video mode

Venturing into video creation with your Nikon Z series camera? Before starting, understand key display information like resolution, frame rate, codecs, and focus mode. Experiment with your camera menu for better understanding.

Ep 2: Two Quick and Effective Setups for Video Filming

Discover two handy video settings in just two minutes! Set frame size and rate, adjust shutter speed, use auto white balance and ISO. Achieve accurate focus with wide-area AF or auto-area AF. Experiment with higher resolutions and the Rich Tone Portrait picture control option.

Ep 3: All About Frame Size and Frame Rate

Discover the fundamentals of video making: frame size and frame rate. Full HD and 4K offer different levels of detail. Optimal frame rates like 24 fps create a smooth look, while higher rates enable slow-motion effects.

Ep 4: ISO and Shutter Speed Setup

Explore the ISO value and shutter speed setup for video filming. Learn how ISO improves image quality with minimal noise and discover the optimal shutter speed settings to avoid choppiness and achieve a natural look. The importance of using neutral density filters to prevent overexposure will be covered too. Get ready to enhance your filming skills!

Ep 5: Choose the Most Suitable Focus Mode & AF-area Mode

Discover the most ideal focus mode and AF-area mode settings to seamless filming. Perfect for elevating your video projects, Alex will also share tips on using advanced models like the Nikon Z 8 to access enhanced features. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, this video will help you achieve precise autofocus for any filming scenario.

Explore essential tools to enhance exposure and composition in filmmaking. Begin with the waveform, crucial for analyzing brightness and preventing exposure errors. The "ETTL" technique for cleaner footage and zebra patterns to spot overexposed areas will also be covered. Finally, the framing grid to improve composition using classic guidelines will be highlighted. Mastering these tools, especially the waveform, will elevate your filmmaking skills. Happy filming!
